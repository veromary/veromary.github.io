Publishers

Here's my short list of favourite publishers and book shops online:
  • Cardinal Newman Faith Resources - My local Catholic book shop. Runs on a shoe-string doing wonderful work getting catechetical supplies out to teachers and families.
  • News Weekly Books or Freedom Publishing. Also focusses on social issues. Based in Melbourne, Australia.
  • John XXIII Bookshop - Also based in Melbourne.
  • Saint Michael's Abbey Press - Benedictine abbey of St. Michael at Farnborough in England, founded from the French Abbey of Solesmes in 1895. Focuses on Liturgy, Gregorian Chant and art. Also sells beautiful cards, rosaries, honey, balm and CDs.
  • Ignatius Press
  • Sophia Institute
  • Angelus Press - Great Daily Missal for the Traditional Latin Mass.
  • Gracewing - an English press that has a book on Caroline Chisholm, a Catholic lady in Australia's difficult beginnings.
  • IHS Press - dedicated to books on the social teaching of the Catholic Church.
  • St Joan of Arc Books - New and second hand Catholic books.

Books

There are a few books I've printed out from texts available on the internet. The smaller books are easily stapled into one signature, larger books are glued to simulate perfect binding. They are printed on regular 80gsm copy paper on a laser printer, so they'll last. Here's the list:
  • A Brief History of England by G.K. Chesterton
  • Calvary and the Mass: a missal companion, by F.J. Sheen
  • Memoir of Father (Saint) Vincent de Paul
  • Around the world with the Trapp Family (without pictures), Maria von Trapp
  • St. Thomas Aquinas by G. K. Chesterton
If you find a certain book you'd like through Project Gutenberg or the EWTN library, or any other source of text files, I might be able to print it for you.
