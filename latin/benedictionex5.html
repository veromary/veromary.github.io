Word list:

  1. quae coeli pandis ostium.
  2. da robur.
  3. fer auxilium.
  4. qui vitam sine termino donet.
  5. audi nos, exaudi nos.
  6. qui tollis peccata mundi.
  7. tantum Sacramentum veneremur.
  8. praestet fides supplementum.
  9. panem de coelo praestitisti.
  10. omne delectamentum habentem.
  11. qui memoriam reliquisti.
  12. tribue ita nos mysteria venerari.
  13. ut redemptionis tuae fructum sentiamus.
  14. adoremus sanctissimum Sacramentum.
  15. laudate Dominum.
  16. laudate eum.

The word-for-word translation:

1. quae who pandis openest ostium the gate coeli of heaven. 2. da give robur strength. 3. fer bring auxilium help. 4. qui and may he donet give vitam life sine termino without end. 5. audi hear nos us, exaudi hear graciously nos us. 6. qui who tollis takest away peccata the sins mundi of the world. 7. veneremur let us venerate tantum Sacramentum so great a Sacrament. 8. fides let faith praestet offer supplementum a supply. 9. praestitisti thou gavest panem bread de coelo from heaven. 10. habentem having omne delectamentum every delight. 11. qui who reliquisti hast left memoriam a memorial. 12. tribue grant nos that we ita venerari may so venerate mysteria the mysteries. 13. ut that sentiamus we may experience fructum the fruit tuae redemptionis of thy redemption. 14. adoremus let us adore sanctissimum Sacramentum the most holy Sacrament. 15. laudate praise ye Dominum the Lord. 16. laudate praise ye eum him.
