Preposition and noun

  1. In saecula.
  2. In aeternum.
  3. Super nos.
  4. Sine termino.
  5. In patria.
  6. De coelis.
  7. Pro nobis.
  8. Sine labe.
  9. Ab Utroque.
  10. De coelo.
  11. In se.
  12. Sub Sacramento.
  13. In nobis.
  14. In principio.

The idea is to be familiar with these phrases.

Here are the translations:

ACCUSATIVE CASE. 1. In saecula unto ages. 2. In aeternum for ever. 3. Super nos upon us.

ABLATIVE CASE. 4. Sine termino without end. 5. In patria in our native home. 6. De coelis of heaven. 7. Pro nobis for us. 8. Sine labe without stain. 9. Ab Utroque from Both. 10. De coelo from heaven. 11. In se in itself. 12. Sub Sacramento under the Sacrament. 13. In nobis within us. 14. In principio in the beginning,
